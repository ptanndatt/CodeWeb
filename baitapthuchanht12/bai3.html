One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm.Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and relizes the harsh existence of the homeless,and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a greatbook. It is well written and interesting. Other books John Grisham include The Firm. The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
